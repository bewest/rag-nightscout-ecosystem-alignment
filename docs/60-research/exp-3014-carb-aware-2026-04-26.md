# EXP-3014 — Carb-absorption-aware trough proxy (2026-04-26)

**Branch**: `main`
**Code**: `tools/cgmencode/autoresearch_cf/exp_3014_carb_aware.py`
**Inputs**: `externals/experiments/exp-3007_ascent_events.parquet` (17,919 events)
**Tests**: robustness of EXP-3011's (T=+30, M=0.5×) recommendation under more realistic hypo accounting.

## Motivation

EXP-3010's hypo proxy is **worst-case**: it computes
```
cf_trough = cf_peak − [kernel(t_peak+W) − kernel(t_peak)] · smb_cand · isf
```
treating the look-ahead window W=120 min as if BG were in free-fall under residual insulin PK with **zero counter-regulation**: no carb absorption, no hepatic glucose production, no basal recovery. This inflates hypo-rate magnitudes, particularly for meal-context ascents where COB is non-zero.

If the EXP-3011 recommendation only wins under worst-case accounting, it's fragile. EXP-3014 tests whether (T=+30, M=0.5×) survives a more physiological proxy.

## Method — carb-aware correction

Add back BG rise from carb absorption during the look-ahead window:
```
cob_at_peak       = cob_start + carbs_during           (gross upper bound)
absorbed_in_W     = cob_at_peak · (W / AT)              AT = 180 min (default)
bg_offset_carbs   = absorbed_in_W · (ISF/CR)            ≈ 4 mg/dL/g (1800/450 rule)

cf_trough_realistic = cf_trough_worst_case + bg_offset_carbs
```

EGP omitted: in 2-hour ascent windows the basal-insulin/EGP cancellation is ~zero net drift, and the carb signal dominates. Future work could add EGP for fasting-ascent subsets.

## Results

| Controller | Model | T* | M* | Δoversht | Δhypo |
|---|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Loop | naive (EXP-3010/3011) | 30 | 0.5 | **−1.62 pp** | −3.86 pp |
| Loop | **carb-aware (EXP-3014)** | **30** | **0.5** | **−1.62 pp** | **−3.33 pp** |
| Trio | naive | 30 | 0.5 | −2.28 pp | −5.87 pp |
| Trio | **carb-aware** | **30** | **0.5** | **−2.28 pp** | **−3.21 pp** |
| OpenAPS (AAPS) | naive | 30 | 3.0× | −0.27 pp | +0.48 pp |
| OpenAPS (AAPS) | carb-aware | 30 | 3.0× | −0.27 pp | +0.48 pp |

### Key observations

1. **The (T=+30, M=0.5×) recommendation is robust.** Both Loop and Trio retain the same optimal grid point under carb-aware accounting. The recommendation does not flip.

2. **Δoversht is identical** across models. The carb-aware correction only touches the trough side; the peak side (overshoot) was never affected by the worst-case assumption.

3. **Loop's hypo benefit shrinks 14 %** (−3.86 → −3.33 pp). Loop ascents have lower mean COB at peak (Loop's bolus-on-carbs is heavier upfront, less COB residual at velocity peak), so the carb correction is small.

4. **Trio's hypo benefit shrinks 45 %** (−5.87 → −3.21 pp). Trio's oref1 SMB strategy fires repeatedly during meal-driven ascents → higher COB residual at peak → larger carb correction. The naive proxy substantially over-credited Trio's hypo improvement.

5. **OpenAPS unchanged**: AAPS-oref0 events have near-zero SMB activity, so the trough proxy mechanism is essentially inactive; carb correction has nothing to act on.

## Implications

### For the Phase 2 closeout claim

The headline (T=+30, M=0.5×) **directional finding is preserved**, but the magnitude of the hypo-improvement claim should be:

| Controller | Naive Δhypo | **Carb-aware Δhypo** | Updated claim |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Loop | −3.86 pp | **−3.33 pp** | "~3 pp hypo reduction" |
| Trio | −5.87 pp | **−3.21 pp** | "~3 pp hypo reduction" |

Previous closeout document and ledger should be annotated: Trio's claimed hypo improvement was inflated; the realistic figure is ~half. The Pareto-optimality conclusion (improvement on **both** axes) is unaffected.

### For autoresearch fitness

The v2 score function should adopt the carb-aware proxy as the default. The naive worst-case can be retained as a `--proxy=worst_case` opt-in for sensitivity analyses, but the carb-aware proxy gives more honest tuning gradients.

### Caveats and limits

- COB upper-bound (`cob_start + carbs_during`) over-counts carbs that have already been absorbed pre-peak. This makes the carb correction itself an **over-correction**, suggesting the true Δhypo lies between naive (−5.87 pp) and carb-aware (−3.21 pp) for Trio. Likely closer to carb-aware since most carbs in 30-60 min ascents are still absorbing at peak.
- The 4 mg/dL/g ISF/CR ratio is population-typical but varies 2–8 mg/dL/g per patient. Per-patient CR refinement is queued for a future EXP if the program continues.
- AT=180 min (3h) is conservative; fast-acting carbs (juice corrections) absorb in ~60 min. Could differentiate by carb type, but cob_start doesn't distinguish.
- EGP omitted. In hypo-prone fasted contexts, EGP would add ~30-50 mg/dL over W=120 min, further reducing hypo claims. Ascent context masks this; minor.

## Verdict

**`recommendation_robust_to_carb_correction`** — (T=+30, M=0.5×) retained as Pareto-optimal for Loop and Trio. Trio's claimed hypo improvement should be revised down ~45 % (−5.87 → −3.21 pp); Loop's by ~14 % (−3.86 → −3.33 pp). Direction and rank-order preserved.
